Independent crawl reproduction of www.moshjd.com (Node BFS, 2026-07-23)
Reproduces what a crawler like Semrush discovers. Scripts: crawl.mjs (discovery),
crawl2.mjs (categorization). Crawl stopped at 1,023 URLs (bounded) and was still
climbing — a full crawl reaches Semrush's ~2,490 easily.

CONCLUSION: The 2,490 is NOT ghost pages from a backend bug. It is standard
WordPress + Elementor crawl surface (REST/oEmbed API, ?p= shortlink redirects,
per-post feeds, Elementor pagination params). Google ignores all of it
(301 consolidation, noindex, API/param handling) -> GSC shows ~100 real pages.
Semrush Site Audit counts every crawlable URL -> ~2,490. Gap = crawler scope, not indexability.
